Token Summary

FISC is issued by FISC Global Foundation Ltd who is also the counterpart of Dubai FIL mining machine node and is based on the Binance Smart Chain (BSC) on-chain mining (Dapp) tokenized system.

FISC is a distributed computing power tokenized tool based on FIL physical mining machine computing power. FISC's real-time liquidity will correspond to the real 5P computing power and this allows users to conduct FIL mining by also staking FISC and obtain FIL tokens as reward.

Mining Model

FISC allocates 60% of the total daily output of FIL into the stake pool, and users mine FIL by staking FISC.
The total amount of FIL actually mined by the daily mining farm's 5P computing power is distributed as follows:

Community Program rewards —
6% (10% of revenue, 60%*10%)

DEX (PancakeSwap) — 10%

Affiliate Program rewards —12%
(20% of revenue, 60%*20%)

Staked token mining — 60%
(distributed depending on the computing power)

- After the public sales, an initial liquidity of 10,000 FISC tokens paired with $500,000 value of FILECOIN will be added into PancakeSwap LP.

- The daily generated 7% of minted FILECOIN will be added to the same PancakeSwap liquidity pool (FISC-FIL) following a 50% allocation rule.

- Pancake Swap is open for trading 60 days after the launching of FISC staking.

Destruction pool — 10%

Mining machine depreciation and maintenance — 20%

Features Of Economic Models

  • Destroy Deflation

    The economic model design of FISC, 5% of all revenue generated by computing power will continue to burn FISC, getting rid of traditional computing power currency models such ast Bitcoin and Ethereum, and integrating unlimited mining revenue into limited computing power.

  • Liquidity Premium

    FISC is integrated with real and effective Filecoin full storage mining computing power, FISC will have a higher liquidity premium. The issuance of each FISC means that the corresponding FISC standard computing power will be added to the mining pool. In this way, the value of FISC is guaranteed to be stable.

  • Long Term Value Sustainability

    Since the launch of the FIL mainnet, the price of Filecoin's single ‘T’ has continued to rise. We have reason to believe that as the FIL computing power market grows, the underlying price of FISC will also continue to grow.

  • Dividend Mechanism

    The FISC dividend pool has set up a 100% guaranteed mechanism. Regardless of the FISC staking rate, the dividend pool will continue to distribute 100% of the income equally to all stakers.

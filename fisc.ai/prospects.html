Sparkling Programme

Since FISC will only be issued when the effective standard full storage power is obtained, Filecoin miners around the world can contribute qualified mining power to FISC in exchange for newly issued FISC.

The Sparkling Programme aims to unite effective miner nodes, under the common vision of FISC, to establish more valuable mining services for miners and investors.

As long as the miner node can prove that it has ownership and control of the contributed mining power, passed the risk assessment, and contribution of the mining rate is higher than the average Filecoin mining, the FISC team will take this into consideration. Anyone can participate in the FISC Startup Plan if they meet the standard requirements.

Including every 1T of computing power that enters the Sparkling Programme and every FISC casting will be announced to the community in the form of a third-party audit report, and will be monitored by the community.
